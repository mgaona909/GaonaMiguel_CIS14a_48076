Our Company

We here at Gaona's Gadgets love all things tech. We carry all kinds gadgets the help make your life easier. Desktops, laptops, mobile devices, or wearables; you name it, we have it. We also offer tech support and repair services to help our customers when something goes wrong with their devices. Whether you are a tech newbie who needs help setting up their new device or a tech guru who needs help on more advanced problems, we are here to help.

Our History

Gaona's Gadgets started off as a small collection of assorted peices of technology assembled by our founder, Miguel Gaona. As his collection grew, he became inspired to share his love of technology with others. Thus, Gaona's Gadgets was born.

A message from our President

As founder and President of Gaona's Gadgets, it is my pleasure to bring quality products to our customers. Asa tech enthusiast myself, I would not sell anything to my customers that I wouldn't use personally. That is why I, alongside my employees, personally test out each new device that we plan to sell. That way we can ensure that our customers receive only the best products.

-Miguel Gaona
