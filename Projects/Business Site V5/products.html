The time is now! The much anticipated Moto 360 smartwatch by Motorola is here. Powered by Android Wear, this watch is both functional and fashionable. Notifications from your phone are displayed on the watch, which you can then scroll through directly on the watch. With the built in microphone, you can perform quick Google searches and even respond to text messages from your wrist.

The Nexus 6 is Motorola's latest and greatest phone. As part of Google's Nexus line, it is powered by the latest version of Android (Android 5.0 Lollipop). Boasting top of the line specs, this phone is guaranteed to turn some heads.

Released alongside the Nexus 6 is its bigger brother, the Nexus 9. This powerful tablet is powered by the same version of the Andorid operating system as The Nexus 6 and shows it off on a beautifully vibrant 8.9 inch screen. This tablet also marks Google's first attempt at making a more premium feeling device.
